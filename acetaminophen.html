Acetaminophen Guide

Acetaminophen is commonly used for fever and pain relief. Dosing is based on your child's weight. Always double-check the concentration on the medication bottle and use the dosing device that comes with the product.

Common Infant Products

When to Use

  • Fever or discomfort associated with mild illnesses.
  • Pain after immunizations or minor injuries.
  • As directed by your pediatrician for teething discomfort.

Safety Tips

  • Do not exceed 5 doses in 24 hours.
  • Allow at least 4 hours between doses for infants 2-6 months old.
  • Keep a log of the time and dose to avoid accidental repeat dosing.
  • Contact a healthcare provider for children under 2 months of age with a fever.
