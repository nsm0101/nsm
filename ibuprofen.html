Ibuprofen Guide

Ibuprofen helps reduce fever, pain, and inflammation. Use ibuprofen only for children older than 6 months unless directed by a healthcare provider.

Common Pediatric Products

Common Uses

  • Fever or pain associated with viral illnesses.
  • Pain and swelling from sprains or strains.
  • Discomfort associated with ear infections or sore throat.

Safety Tips

  • Give with food or milk to minimize stomach upset.
  • Avoid use in children who are dehydrated or vomiting frequently.
  • Do not combine with other anti-inflammatory medicines unless advised.
  • Spacing between doses should be at least 6 hours.
